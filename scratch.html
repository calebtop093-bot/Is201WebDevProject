Favorite Hikes in Utah: The mountains here in Utah have been a big passion of mine for quite a while now. Below is a list of my favorite hikes and their distances.

  1. Lake Blanche
    • Located in Salt Lake; approximately 6.4 miles.
    • Features three different alpine lakes.
  2. Khyv Peak
    • Located in Provo; approximately 7.2 miles.
    • Offers views of Provo city and surrounding mountains.
  3. Ridge Trail
    • Located in Provo; approximately 5.8 miles.
    • Popular for hiking and mountain biking with views of Mount Timpanogos.
